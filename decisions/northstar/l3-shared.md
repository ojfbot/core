# Northstar — shared ojfbot ⊕ selfco (L3)

**Vision.** The apex both ventures ladder into. ojfbot (the dev platform + Frame OS fleet) and
selfco (the LLM knowledge system) are two halves of one bet: an AI-native cluster where shipping
product and accumulating legible knowledge are the same loop. The shared north star is that this
cluster *works* (P1) and that its own progress is *honestly knowable* (P2) — the second being the
precondition for trusting the first.

## P1 — A working AI-native product cluster exists

The fleet and selfco are demoable and used, not aspirational. Movement here is the weighted result
of the L2 ventures (`l2-ojfbot`, `l2-selfco`) advancing their own delivery properties.

## P2 — Work is legible and self-measuring

The cluster can tell the truth about what it has done. This is the property the provenance-hardening
and northstar work directly advance: honest session pickup, daily work framed against measurable
properties, recorded (not remembered) movement. Without P2, P1's numbers can't be trusted.
